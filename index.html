PopCat Clicker
Cute neon cat clicker game – click, collect & have fun!

About the Game

PopCat Clicker is a colorful casual game for kids and families.

Click the cute cat to make shiny items pop out, collect them, and turn them into coins inside the game!

Main Features

  • Simple and relaxing clicker gameplay
  • Neon cat graphics and juicy visual effects
  • Collect potions, gems, stars and more
  • Exchange items for coins inside the shop
  • Child-friendly design, no violence
