About the Game

PopCat Clicker is a colorful casual game for kids and families.

Click the cute cat to make shiny items pop out, collect them, and turn them into coins inside the game!

Main Features

  • Simple and relaxing tap-to-play clicker gameplay
  • Neon cat graphics and juicy particle effects
  • Collect potions, gems, paws, stars and more
  • Exchange items for coins inside the jewellery shop
  • Child-friendly design, no violence or chat

Privacy & Safety

The game is built with privacy first: no accounts, no personal data collection, and only child-appropriate ads. You can read the full privacy policy here: Privacy Policy.
